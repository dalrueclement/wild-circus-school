History

Founded in 1990 by Clément Dalrue, it demonstrates above all a passion. They imagine an exceptional event where, for the first time, young circus artists will have the opportunity to present their art in front of an international jury and a curious and enthusiastic audience.

Animals

The performing animals are obviously artists at least of the performers. They play the comedy or make the show and are sometimes even elevated to the rank of star of the small and big screen like our Mushou, our panther that makes the happiness of the adult and young.

acrobatics

Acrobatics is a complete art that develops agility, flexibility, tone, and balance while respecting the body, supports and joints. It contributes in particular to consolidate the consciousness of body and space, strengthens concentration, stimulates emulation and transcends self, and channels energy.
